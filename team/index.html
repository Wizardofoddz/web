Blocktech Culture

The team at Blocktech believes in practising sustainability and drives toward a zero-waste lifestyle. As Earth transitions away from a scarce way of life over the next twenty years to one of abundance, Blocktech aims to build our products with a passion for empowering humans to mindfully evolve.

BlockHouse

BlockHouse is the physical hub where minds meet daily to build out Blocktech's future. Situated in sunny San Diego with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, conjuring inspiration is a very fluid and tranquil process. Team members from around the World visit to plug in with the team face to face.

Community Outreach

Actions speak louder than words. Blocktech believes in giving back to the community. Doing good unto the Universes is second nature here. Giving back to our community and World serve to drive mass adoption of the Blocktech culture and way of life.
