Distributed applications will become one of the most collaborative monuments of our generation.

Powerful next generation distributed applications will bridge the gap between connecting the crowd to solving many of the World's largest challenges. Distributing the work of otherwise unimaginable tasks and storing the result on blockchains will be what give the crypto space validity and drive mass long term adoption.  Tapping into the collective brain power of the crowd will give blockchains utility never once realized. Blocktech's vision for Distributed Applications center around building the engines of tomorrow that will harness and display the collective energy of the masses.

Alexandria - Keeping history honest

Alexandria records unfolding events scraped from social media and traditional news outlets and stores this data on the blockchain keeping the evolution of the story intact.

Alexandria preserves the reporting of events forever, and prevents them from being altered after the fact. It records the reporting of unfolding events from self-published social media as well as traditional news outlets and keeps the evolution of the story intact. Named for the Library of Alexandria because of the tales that caused it to be known as a symbol of the destruction of ancient knowledge, Alexandria is intended to preserve the integrity of the historical record.

In a World where seeing is believing, Alexandria taps into the collective consciousness of the crowd as current events unfold rather than relying on the word of a traditional few. Alexandria's visual word cloud and timeline slider empower the World to discover new webs of connection and thought that may otherwise have remained unrealized.

Coinvance - Collaboration for distributed co-creation.

Distributed teams are becoming masters of co-creation out of necessity. The next generation of collaboration tools will embrace the naturally evolving development cycles of everyday communication. A desktop work space of the future will enable collaboration to take place as a layer that sits atop distributed applications and the blockchains in which they represent. Chat becomes something that is immediately actionable and shared at the organizational level and beyond.
