ΛLΞXΛNDRIΛ is a distributed library for sharing and preserving art, history and culture. One can picture it as essentially the same thing as youtube, soundcloud or netflix, but by making use of various P2P networking technologies, the platform relies on no servers! Users can self-publish anything they'd like; videos, songs, books, 3d printables, recipes, and more, and they (and only they) have complete control of how their works are published.

By integrating bittorrent technology, HD media can be streamed in the browser, and because there is no server or bandwidth overhead, we don't need to make users watch ads. With the Florincoin blockchain at its heart, a cryptocurrency payments layer is built right in. Content creators will be able to monetize their work in a variety of ways - from free with optional tipping, to microsubscription streaming to pay-per-play.
