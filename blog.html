Crypto Consultancy with a Mission

Let’s take a minute to rewind to the mid-nineties, a time when personal computers and the internet were becoming more easily accessible to the general public. Popular internet service providers such as America Online and Compuserve began to flourish and 28.8kb/s modems were bundled with a flood of free CD-Roms and easy to understand guided user interfaces. Intrigued end users were beginning to sign online and explore the possibility of connecting with people, places, and things from around the World in a completely new way. The apple of information had received its first bite from the masses. With an intrigued third eye, tech entrepreneurs began popping up and the foundation for the first tech boom was paved. As the basic needs of the public met the internet, popular household names like Yahoo, Amazon, Ebay, Paypal saw their first breaths of life.

Now fast forward twenty years later to present day. Technology has reached every facet of life and the way we as a civilization communicate and network has been forever changed. Knowledge is truth and the technology that has been brought to the surface over the past twenty years reflects a newfound understanding for the way humans mindfully evolve. Peer to peer and social are the new norm. As civilization goes through the growing pains of coming to a new understanding of the utopia that is possible right here on Earth, old systems begin to collapse and are replaced by more efficient ones that match the needs of a more interconnected World.

“One of the most critical vulnerabilities that presently threaten this possibility of a realized singularity is the centralization of vital services and technology.”

Putting trust into systems that at their core are broken and manipulated for nefarious reasons has put our digital revolution at risk from realizing its next cyclical evolution. Crypto has naturally begun evolving into the realized distributed solution. The wave of decentralization of all things internet is flourishing with tidal force and the pioneers of this space realize the mission they have been given to better mankind.

Blockchains are the DNA of the crypto space and as entrepreneurs begin to assemble these building blocks, the body of this space is taking shape in a beautifully artistic way. The greater part of society will begin to awaken to this next generation of distributed, autonomous, everything. With this great awakening comes a new set of needs that have not been paralleled since the mid nineties. Blockchains serve as much more than just vehicles for financial transactions and settlement. When given utility, blockchains serve to become one of the most disruptive monuments of our generation.

Blockchain Technology Group is an integrated, or “full stack,” company with a vision for blockchain applications far beyond financial remittance. Blocktech is working on an unalterable ledger of history, a new kind of lower-cost, higher speed media distribution, safe and inexpensive securities and commodities trading, crowd-sourced hardware development and much more.

Ryan Keenan, Ideation
